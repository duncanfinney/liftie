Snow Report

Report for: Friday, March 7th

Mount Peter will operate today from 10 a.m.-8 p.m.

We are skiing and riding on a machine groomed frozen granular surface. The forecast today is calling for clear skies with a high of 44 degrees. We are expecting a windy day so don’t forget that outer layer!

Tubing is open today, head over to shop.mtpeter.com to get your tickets!

 
 

Join our Email Club for up-to-date information.

You can also follow us on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter and YouTube.
